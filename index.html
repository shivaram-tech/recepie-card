Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish layered with marinated meat and aromatic spices, celebrated for its rich flavors and vibrant colors.

INGREDIENTS:

  • Chicken - 500g, cut into pieces
  • Basmati Rice - 2 cups, soake
  • Yogurt - 1/2 cup
  • Onions - 2, sliced
  • Tomatoes - 1, chopped (optional)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Mint and Coriander Leaves - a handful, chopped
  • Biryani Masala or Garam Masala - 2 tablespoons
  • Whole Spices - bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon
  • Turmeric Powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red Chili Powder - 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Cooking Oil or Ghee - 2 tablespoons

PREPARATION:

  • Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

  • Cook Rice: Parboil basmati rice with whole spices like bay leaf, cardamom, and cloves until it’s halfway done, then drain.

  • Saute Onion & Spices: In a large pot, fry sliced onions until golden brown. Add marinated chicken and cook until it’s tender and spices are well blended.

  • Layer Rice and Chicken: Spread half of the parboiled rice over the chicken. Add some fried onions, mint, and coriander. Repeat with another layer of rice and herbs.

  • Dum Cook: Seal the pot with a lid, cook on low heat for about 20 minutes until the rice and chicken are fully cooked and aromatic.

  • Serve: Gently mix and serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy!
